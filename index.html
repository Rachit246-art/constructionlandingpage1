Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the construction cost in Bangalore?

Construction costs in Bangalore typically range from ₹1,800 to ₹3,500 per sq ft depending on the quality of materials, location, and complexity of the design. We provide detailed quotations to give you an exact estimate.

Do you provide turnkey solutions?

Yes, we specialize in turnkey solutions where we manage everything from planning and approvals to material procurement, construction, and interior finishing.

How long does construction take?

A typical luxury villa construction takes between 10 to 14 months, depending on the scale and complexity of the project. We provide a detailed project timeline at the start.

Can designs be customized?

Absolutely! Every project we undertake is unique and customized to the client's specific needs, lifestyle, and aesthetic preferences.

Do you assist with approvals?

Yes, we assist our clients with all necessary government approvals, plan sanctions, and documentation required for the construction process.
